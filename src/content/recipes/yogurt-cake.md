## Ingredients

- 1 pot plain yogurt
- 2 pots sugar
- 3 pots flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 lemon

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 180°C and line a loaf tin.
2. Whisk the yogurt, sugar, eggs, and lemon zest together, then fold in the flour.
3. Bake until a skewer comes out clean.
