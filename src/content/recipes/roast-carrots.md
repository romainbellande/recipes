## Ingredients

- 800 g carrots
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 lemon
- Salt

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220°C and cut the carrots into even pieces.
2. Toss with oil, cumin, and salt.
3. Roast for 25 to 30 minutes, then squeeze over lemon.
