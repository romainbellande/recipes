## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 handful soft herbs
- Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt.
2. Melt the butter in a low pan, add the eggs, and stir slowly until softly set.
3. Fold through the herbs and season with pepper.
