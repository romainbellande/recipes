## Ingredients

- 2 tins butter beans
- 200 g greens
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 lemon
- 3 tbsp olive oil

## Method

1. Soften the garlic in oil over medium heat.
2. Add the beans with a splash of water and simmer until glossy.
3. Stir in the greens to wilt, then finish with lemon.
