## Ingredients

- 200 g dark chocolate
- 4 eggs
- 300 ml double cream
- 1 pinch salt

## Method

1. Melt the chocolate and let it cool slightly.
2. Whisk the egg whites with salt until they hold soft peaks.
3. Fold the yolks and cream into the chocolate, then gently fold in the whites.
4. Chill for at least 2 hours.
