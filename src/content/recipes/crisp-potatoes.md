## Ingredients

- 1 kg potatoes
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt
- 1 sprig rosemary

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220°C and cut the potatoes into chunks.
2. Boil for 8 minutes, drain, and shake the pan to roughen the edges.
3. Toss with oil and rosemary, then roast until deeply golden.
