## Ingredients

- 100 g rolled oats
- 500 ml milk or water
- 1 apple, grated
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp toasted seeds

## Method

1. Simmer the oats, milk, apple, and cinnamon for 8 minutes, stirring often.
2. Divide between bowls and scatter over the seeds.
