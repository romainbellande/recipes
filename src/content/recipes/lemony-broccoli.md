## Ingredients

- 2 heads broccoli
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220°C and cut the broccoli into florets.
2. Toss with oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast until charred at the edges, then finish with lemon.
