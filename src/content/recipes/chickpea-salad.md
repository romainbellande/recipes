## Ingredients

- 2 tins chickpeas
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 cucumber
- 200 g tomatoes
- 1 lemon

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220°C and dry the chickpeas well.
2. Toss chickpeas with oil and cumin, then roast until crisp.
3. Chop the vegetables, dress with lemon, and top with warm chickpeas.
